• One plate per line, followed by one or more wells.
  • Type or paste the set of well identifiers (plate number and well name).
  • Separate every element on the line with whitespace, commas, or semicolons.
  • The first well on the line does not need a separator from the plate number.
  • Case (uppercase or lowercase) does not matters.
  • Click the "Find Wells" button. (Or press the Tab key to move focus to the button, and then press Spacebar or Enter.)
  • Examples: 
    • 50A6
    • 50a6 b10 c20
    • 50a6,b10,c20
    • 50 A6 A7 A8
