You can easily look up the contents of wells in the ICCB-L libraries collection in various ways:

  • To look up the contents of a single well, enter in the plate number and well name in the Plate/Well text boxes in the left menu pane.
  • Or, click on the "Find Wells" link in the left menu pane to get to the Well Finder page.

On the Well Finder page, you can enter in the coordinates for a single well or for multiple wells. The Well Finder page contains further instructions explaining how the well coordinates should be specified.

As a Libraries Administrator, you can view click the "Find Well Volumes" button to view volume information for each of the library plate copies of the specified wells.
